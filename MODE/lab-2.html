Xamarin Supports multiple languages as it is built in to Visual Studio.
Xamarin also has built in localization support across multiple platforms but transcription is not guaranteed to be 100% accurate without paying for the service of actual translators to convert it.

Xamarin has built in certification and connectivity with Android as long as you are a registered developer.
iOS requires you to package your app first and then publish it manually. The same for Windows apps.
All of the above require you to be a registered developer and that your app does not break the ToS.

Xamarin has a built in Android Emulator as well as an iOS simulator.
