Topic 1 - Stephen Hawking

Stephen Hawking, a world renowned Theoretical Physicist, Cosmologist, and Author. Also the Director of Research at the Centre for Theoretical Cosmology within the University of Cambridge. One of his scientific works includes a collaboration with Roger Penrose on gravitational singularity theorems in the framework of general relativity.


Another, his theoretical prediction that black holes emit radiation, which is now commonly referred to as Hawking radiation. Hawking was the first to set out a theory of cosmology explained by a union of the general theory of relativity and quantum mechanics. He was also a vigorous supporter of the many-worlds interpretation of quantum mechanics.


He is perhaps best known for his book "A Brief History of Time", in which he talks about basic concepts like space and time, basic building blocks that make up the universe (such as quarks) and the fundamental forces that govern it (such as gravity).
